RESULT ANALYSIS OF AIEEE

AIEEE was divided into 2 parts- Paper 1 and Paper 2
Paper 1 had 3 sections namely; Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics. Each section comprised of objective type questions with 4 choices. Paper 1 has a negative marking system where 0.25 marks will deducted for each wrong answer.
Paper 2 had 3 sections namely; Drawing, Mathematics and Aptitude. Aptitude section comprised of drawing based questions while mathematics and drawing section had objective type questions.
  • Physics and Chemistry - 150 multiple choice based questions with equal weightage to Physics and Chemistry - Total marks 450
  • Mathemtics - 75 objective type questions - Total marks 225
  • Aptitude Test Part I - objective type questions - Total marks 150
  • Aptitude Test Part II - questions to test drawing aptitude - Total marks 75
