Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidates must be born on or after July 1, 1993.
  • Candidates must have passed 10+2 or equivalent form a recognized board.
  • They must have secured a minimum of 60% in aggregate in Maths, Physics and Chemistry.
  • A minimum of 55% is mandatory in the individual subjects of Maths, Physics and Chemistry
  • Candidates with a minimum of 60% in a three year diploma in engineering awarded by any State Board of Technical Education are also eligible
