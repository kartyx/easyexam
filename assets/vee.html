Eligibility Criteria

Candidate who wants to take up the entrance exam VEE must fulfill the following eligibility criteria as per the university norms:-
  • Candidates who are residents of India or Non resident Indians whose date of birth falls on or after July 1,1989 are eligible to apply for entrance exam VEE.
  • Candidate should have studied in full time formal education in their schooling.

For B.E programs:

Candidate should have pass in 10+2 or equivalent exam from recognized board with minimum of 50% marks in aggregate in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics.
  • For B.Pharm and Pharm.D programs: Candidate should have pass in 10+2 or equivalent exam from recognized board with minimum of 50% marks in aggregate in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics or Biology.
  • For MBA and MCA: Candidate should have pass in any degree with 10+2+3 pattern or its equivalent from recognized university with minimum of 45% aggregate.
  • For M.Pharm and Pharm.D (Post Baccalaureate): Candidate should have passed in B.Pharm with 10+2+4 pattern.
  • Candidates appearing for +2 examinations and final year degree examination are also eligible to apply for entrance test VEE.
