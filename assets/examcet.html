Eligibility Criteria

  • ACandidates must be Indian Nationals or Persons of India Origin (PIO) or Overseas Citizen of India (OCI) card holders.
  • Applicants should be from the state of Andhra Pradesh and should satisfy the Local / Non-Local status – as per the requirements of the Andhra Pradesh Education Institutions order, 1974.
  • Candidates should have passed the 10+2 with Mathematics and Physics along with Chemistry / Bio-Technology / Biology as the optional subjects.
  • All the applicants should have attained a minimum of 45% in 10+2 and in the case of reserved category 40% is required to meet the EAMCET 2014 Eligibility Criteria.
  • Age Limit: Candidates should have completed 16 years of age as on 31st December, 2014 and there is no upper age limit.
