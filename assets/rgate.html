RESULT ANALYSIS OF GETE

  • The exam was conducted at 660 centers across 26 states and union territories in India. As many as 10,33,625 aspirants registered for the exam, however, 8,89,156 candidates (86.02 per cent) actually took the exam.
  • Out of the total registered candidates, 86 per cent male (out of the total male candidates who registered) and 85 per cent female registrants (out of the total female candidates who registered) appeared for the examination. Of the total candidates who registered for the exam, 30.17 per cent were females while the rest were male candidates and others.
