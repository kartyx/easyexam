Eligibility criteria:

  • There is no minimum eligibility criterion for taking IELTS test. Any person who wishes to pursue education or work opportunities in English speaking countries can apply for theIELTS test.
  • According to the test pattern of IELTS,the test is conducted in two versions - IELTS Academic and IELTS General Training. There are no minimum IELTS Eligibility criteria for conducting the test. However, a candidate is required to check the other required eligibility criteria for the organization or institution he/she wishes to be associated with. The aspirant should also be informed about the selection procedureof their respective institutes at the time of registration.
