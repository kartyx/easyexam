Educational Qualification:

  • candidates must have passed Class 10+2 or its equivalent. However, the candidates who are appearing in the 10+2 exam can also apply for AIEEE. The AIEEE Eligibility Criteria will however, also include the admission criteria of the concerned college at the time of admission.
  • Age Bar: Candidates who are born ON or AFTER the first week of October 1989. For the candidates who belong to ST, SC or PWD categories, a 5 years relaxation is provided i.e. who are born on/after first week of October 1984 is ELIGIBLE according to AIEEE 2014 Eligibility Criteria.

Eligibility criteria for the foreign nationals/NRI/PIO:

  • Foreign Nationals / PIOs/ NRIs must have passed the qualifying examination i.e. senior secondary or any other examination equivalent to 12 years of schooling in India with the aggregate of 60% or 6.75 Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) on 10 point Scale in all the subjects.\n" +
  • Indian nationals living abroad must have studied there for minimum 5 years (including the qualifying examination i.e. XI & XII Standards) during the last eight years.
