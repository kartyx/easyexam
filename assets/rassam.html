result analysis of assam jat exam

Assam JAT 2014 Result has been declared on June 5, 2014 at 2 pm. Students who have appeared for the exam on May 28 and 29, 2014 can know their marks through the Assam JAT 2014 Result. The admissions to the various engineering courses will be on the basis of the Assam JAT 2014 Result. Meritorious students will be given admissions to 975 seats in government engineering colleges of Assam, along with seats in eight state universities and private engineering colleges.
Assam JAT 2014 Result:
•	Assam JAT 2014 Result is made on the basis of the ranks obtained in the Assam JAT 2014.
•	Students can check their result by using theirAssam JAT 2014 Roll No.
•	Candidates can apply for admissions to the following engineering colleges on the basis of theAssam JAT 2014 Result:
> Assam Engineering College, Guwahati (420 seats)
> Jorhat Engineering College, Jorhat (315 seats)
> Jorhat Institute of Science & Technology, Jorhat (120 seats)
> Bineswar Brahma Engineering College, Kokrajhar (120 seats)
> Private Engineering Colleges and State Universities of Assam
