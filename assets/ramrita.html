result analysis of amrita university

AEEE 2014 comprised of three parts- Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry. There were 120 questions in total, which were of 360 marks.
subject weightage
maths 50 questions
physics 35 questions
chemistry 35 questions
A large number of students appear for Amrita Engineering Entrance Exam 2014 every year. Last year as well as this year, approximately 33000 + candidates showed up to take the exam. The question paper for AEEE 2014 was of moderate difficulty level. Its difficulty level was slightly below that of JEE (Main) 2014. All topics from Physics, Mathematics and Chemistry were given due weightage. Candidates getting higher than 8000 rank are likely to get a seat in engineering programmes in Amrita University. There were four different sets of question paper. We had 4 versions A, B, C and D. There were same questions in all the sets, only the sequence of questions was different.
