SYLLABUS FOR BEEE

Syllabus:The questions in the entrance test are generally asked from Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics of 10+2 level.
  1. Physics:Definitions of luminous flux, Discovery of radioactivity, Units, Dimensions and Dimensional equations Displacement, Electric current, Photoelectric effect, Semiconductor, Simple harmonic motion, Structure of nuclears, Heat as kinetic energy of molecular motion, Newton's law of motion etc.
  2. Chemistry:Electro chemistry, Ionics equilibria, kinetic theory of gases, Surface chemistry,Nuclear chemistry, Chemical reaction, Chemical Equilibrium, Atomic structure,States of matter, Chemical kinetics, Chemical bonding, Nitrogen family, Metals and metallurgical, Chemistry of carbon compounds, postulates, Periodic classification of elements etc.
  3. Mathematics:Trigonometry, VectorAlgebra, Algebra, Coordinate Geometry, Calculus, Probability are included in the topics of Mathematics.
  4. General Knowledge or aptitude:For Aptitude questions, Reasoning, vocabulary, English usage or Grammar,linguistics etc. should be practiced by the candidates.
