Eligibility Criteria

  • For taking Test of English as a Foreign Language, TOEFL Test no minimum educational qualification is required. The test administrator “Educational Testing Services” recommend that 11th grade level or above is suitable qualification for taking this test.
  • As far as Indian candidates are concerned, ordinarily a pass in 10+2 level of any recognized Indian Board or University is a suitable qualification to take the TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language) test, although the level of the test is little on the higher side.
