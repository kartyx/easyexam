result analysis of BEEE

BEEE 2014 Result has been announced on May 23, 2014. 
Candidates who have appeared for the BEEE 2014 exam on May 4 (offline mode) and May 11 (online mode) can check their Result of BEEE 2014 using their BEEE 2014 Hall Ticket No or Mobile No that was provided in the BEEE 2014 Application Form.
 The BEEE 2014 Result will be the basis for providing admissions to candidates to the various engineering courses at Bharath University.
The university will publish the cutoff list along with the results of BEEE 2014. 
The cut off list will have the names of the candidates who got selected after the entrance examination.
 The candidates whose names appear in the cutoff list will be called for the counselling. 
The final admission is based on the counselling of the candidates.
