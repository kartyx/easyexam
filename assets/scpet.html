Syllabus for CPET:

  • Part I: Mathematics/ Biology/ Biotechnology (100 Questions)
  • Part II: Either of the following combination of subjects:
Marks Allocation
  • Physics & Chemistry/ Physics & Biology/ Physics & Biotechnology (100 Questions)
  • Duration of Exam: 3 Hours
  • Total Number of Questions: 200
  • Total Marks Allotted: 200
Notes:
  • Strategy of Marking:The candidates shall be evaluated for the concerned exam in the following way- 1 mark for every right questions attempted and no marks given for the incorrect answers attempted by them respectively. The candidates will be required to answer the questions on an OMR Sheet as provided for this purpose. There will be four options per question; the candidates will have to choose the most correct option for answer.
  • The paper having wrong questions/ incomplete questions will be adjusted in the total marks for evaluation purposes. The candidates should use only Blue/ Black Ball Point Pens for marking answers in the OMR.
