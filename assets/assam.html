Eligibility Criteria

  • The candidates who wish to get admission for B.Tech must have passed 10+2 from the Assam Higher Secondary Board or any other recognized Boards in the country.
  • The candidate must have scored a minimum aggregate of 50% marks in English, Physics, Chemistry and Maths. The minimum marks requirement for the SC candidates is 45% and for the ST candidates is 40%.
  • The candidate must not be below the age of 17 years or above the age of 21 years in the year when he/she seeks admission.
  • The age limit for the candidates who passed B.Sc. is 22 years. There is an age relaxation of three years for the candidates who fall under SC/ST category.
